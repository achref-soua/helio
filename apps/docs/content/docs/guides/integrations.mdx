---
title: Integrations
description: Sync external platforms into your CDP. Shopify customers and orders today.
---

Connect external platforms under **Settings → Integrations** (admins only).
Their events stream into your CDP as contacts and traits.

## Shopify

Connect a store by pasting its **shop domain** (`acme.myshopify.com`) and the
**webhook signing secret** — the API secret of the Shopify app you use to send
webhooks. Then point your Shopify webhooks at Helio's gateway:

```
POST https://<your-helio-gateway>/webhooks/shopify
```

Subscribe these topics:

| Topic              | Effect                                                           |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `customers/create` | Upserts the customer as a contact                                |
| `customers/update` | Refreshes the contact and its order stats                        |
| `orders/create`    | Upserts the buyer and records the order value as a contact trait |

Every delivery is verified against your secret (`X-Shopify-Hmac-Sha256`, base64
HMAC-SHA256 of the raw body) before anything is written — an unsigned or
mis-signed request is rejected. The shop domain (`X-Shopify-Shop-Domain`)
resolves the connection, so a shop maps to exactly one org and workspace.

Contacts gain `shopify_*` traits (customer id, orders count, total spent, last
order value and currency) you can [segment](/docs/guides/segments) on — for
example "Shopify customers who spent over \$500". Existing contacts are merged,
never downgraded.

<Callout>
  The connection is tenant-isolated like everything else: the signing secret lives in the org-scoped
  table and is never returned to the client after it's saved.
</Callout>

## Salesforce

Connect Salesforce by pasting your **instance URL** (`https://acme.my.salesforce.com`)
and an **access token**. Once connected, every new Helio contact is pushed to
Salesforce as a **Lead** — first/last name and email map across, and the
contact's `company` attribute becomes the Lead's Company (falling back to the
email and `Unknown` when those are missing, since Salesforce requires them).

The push is **best-effort**: it runs through the Salesforce REST API
(`/services/data/v60.0/sobjects/Lead`) after the contact is saved, and a
Salesforce error is recorded on the request trace but never fails the contact
write. The access token is stored in the org-scoped connection and never
returned to the client.

<Callout>
  Token-based connect is the v1 path; an OAuth install flow that refreshes tokens automatically is
  on the [roadmap](https://github.com/achref-soua/helio#roadmap).
</Callout>
