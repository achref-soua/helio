title: CRM-lite
description: Pipelines with a deal board and a task list, all tenant-isolated.
---

Helio includes a lightweight CRM for tracking deals and follow-ups alongside
your marketing — pipelines, stages, a board, and tasks, without the weight of a
full sales suite.

## Pipelines & stages

A **pipeline** models a sales process as an ordered set of **stages** (for
example _Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Won / Lost_). Each stage has a kind that
tells Helio whether reaching it means the deal is open, won, or lost.

## The deal board

Deals are shown on a **kanban board**, one column per stage. Moving a deal
between stages updates its status — a deal **closes** when it reaches a won/lost
stage and **reopens** if it moves back to an open one. Like everything else in
Helio, deals are tenant-isolated by [row-level security](/docs/concepts/tenancy).

## Tasks

A **task** is a to-do — a call, email, meeting, or generic reminder — with a
priority and an optional due date. Tasks can be linked to a contact and/or a
deal, so a follow-up sits next to the opportunity it belongs to.

The task list groups open work by when it's due — **Overdue**, **Today**, and
**Upcoming** — with **No due date** and **Done** below, so the most pressing
follow-ups surface first. Completing a task stamps it done (and you can reopen
it); the grouping is computed in your own time zone. Tasks are tenant-isolated
like everything else.

## API

Pipelines, deals, and tasks are available through the dashboard today. The CRM
data model (pipelines, stages, deals with status, and tasks) is part of the
platform; a meeting scheduler is on the
[roadmap](https://github.com/achref-soua/helio#roadmap).

## Contact detail pages

Every contact's email in the table links to its detail page: profile and
traits, scores and predictions, list memberships, linked deals and tasks,
**notes** (pinned notes stay on top — visible to the whole team), and a
unified activity timeline merging email sends, behavioral events, and
recorded changes. Behavioral events need the analytics store; without it
the page says so in one line and shows everything else.

## Deal detail pages

Click any deal card to open it: stage and owner pickers (every member can
be assigned), **Mark won / Mark lost** with an optional reason that the
history keeps, the linked contact (one click to their page) and company,
tasks, team notes, and the deal's full recorded history — every move,
edit, close, and reopen with who did it and when.

## Companies

**Companies** (in the sidebar) is the B2B account list — name, domain,
industry, website, and live counts of attached contacts and deals. Attach
a contact from its detail page (the company picker in the header) and a
deal from its Linked records card. Deleting a company never deletes its
contacts or deals; they simply detach.
