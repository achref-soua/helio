Helio includes a lightweight CRM for tracking deals alongside your marketing —
pipelines, stages, and a board, without the weight of a full sales suite.

## Pipelines & stages

A **pipeline** models a sales process as an ordered set of **stages** (for
example _Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Won / Lost_). Each stage has a kind that
tells Helio whether reaching it means the deal is open, won, or lost.

## The deal board

Deals are shown on a **kanban board**, one column per stage. Moving a deal
between stages updates its status — a deal **closes** when it reaches a won/lost
stage and **reopens** if it moves back to an open one. Like everything else in
Helio, deals are tenant-isolated by [row-level security](/docs/concepts/tenancy).

## API

Pipelines and deals are available through the dashboard today. The CRM data
model (pipelines, stages, deals with status) is part of the platform; deal
tasks and a meeting scheduler are on the
[roadmap](https://github.com/achref-soua/helio#roadmap).
