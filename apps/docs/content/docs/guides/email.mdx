Helio's email system covers design, personalization, sending, and tracking —
with deliverability hygiene built in.

## The block builder

Compose emails from blocks (text, image, button, divider, columns) with a
server-rendered **live preview** — what you see is exactly what renders, because
the preview uses the same renderer as the send path. Templates are reusable and
versioned per workspace.

### Images

Image blocks take a URL **or an upload from your device** (PNG, JPEG, GIF,
WebP — up to 2 MiB). Uploads are stored in Helio's own database and served from
`/a/<id>` on your instance, so the core profile needs no object store and inbox
clients can always fetch them. Each image block also has **width**,
**alignment**, and **corner-rounding** controls, applied by the same renderer
in the preview and the real send.

## Personalization

Insert contact data with tokens that fall back gracefully when a value is
missing:

```
Hi `{{firstName|there}}`, your `{{attributes.plan|free}}` plan renews soon.
```

The `|` provides a fallback, so an absent `firstName` renders `there` rather
than a blank. Conditional blocks let you show or hide sections based on contact
attributes.

<Callout type="info">
  In the dashboard you write tokens as `{{firstName|there}}` — the backticks
  above are only so they render literally on this page.
</Callout>

## Open & click tracking

- An invisible **open pixel** records opens.
- Links are rewritten through an **HMAC-signed click redirector**, so a tracking
  link can't be tampered with to point somewhere else — the signature binds the
  destination to the specific send.

Both stream engagement events into [analytics](/docs/guides/analytics) and can
drive **wait-for-event** steps in [journeys](/docs/guides/journeys).

## Unsubscribe & preferences

Every marketing email carries a **one-click unsubscribe** (RFC 8058) header and
link, plus a hosted **preference center**. Unsubscribes hit the suppression list
immediately and are honored across all future sends.

## Connect your provider

Each organization sends through **its own** provider, connected once under
**Settings → Provider credentials → Email sending**: native adapters for
Postmark, Resend, and Mailgun, plus SMTP for any relay (Amazon SES, Gmail,
Brevo, your own server — use their SMTP endpoints). The credential carries
your From name and address, is encrypted at rest, and can be verified with
one click before anything is sent. Until a provider is connected, sends use
the deployment's fallback SMTP — which in local development is Mailpit, a
test inbox that delivers nothing.

## Deliverability

- Transactional and marketing streams are kept separate.
- Bounce and complaint webhooks feed the suppression list automatically.
- Helio sends through your relay or provider — it deliberately does **not**
  ship its own MTA, which would be a deliverability trap. In local
  development, all mail is caught by
  [Mailpit](https://mailpit.axllent.org/) so nothing real is sent.

## Natural language → email

The [AI copilot](/docs/guides/ai-copilot) can draft an on-brand email from a
prompt, grounded in your existing templates so the voice matches. The draft
lands in the builder for you to edit before anything is sent.
