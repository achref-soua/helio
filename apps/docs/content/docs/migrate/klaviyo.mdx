---
title: From Klaviyo
description: Export Klaviyo profiles and import them into Helio with consent state preserved.
---

Helio detects Klaviyo profile exports and maps Klaviyo's email consent state to
Helio's suppression list.

## Export from Klaviyo

1. In Klaviyo, go to **Audience → Lists & Segments**.
2. Open the list or segment you want to move and choose **Export** (or
   **Manage → Export list to CSV**).
3. Include the **email**, name, and **email consent / subscription** columns.

## Import into Helio

1. Open **Contacts → Import** in Helio and drop the CSV.
2. Helio detects the Klaviyo format and shows the count of contacts that will be
   imported as **unsubscribed**.
3. Review the validation summary and confirm.

## What carries over

- **Email** and name fields map to the built-in fields.
- Profiles whose email consent is **unsubscribed / suppressed** import as
  `UNSUBSCRIBED` and join the
  [suppression list](/docs/guides/contacts#suppression--privacy).
- Custom profile properties become [attributes](/docs/guides/contacts).

Once imported, rebuild your Klaviyo flows as
[journeys](/docs/guides/journeys) — they run durably on Temporal and survive
worker restarts mid-wait without double-sending.
