**Helio** is an open-source growth platform: unify customer data, segment
anyone, orchestrate journeys across every channel, and let AI do the heavy
lifting. Self-host it, own your data, and pay nothing per contact.

It is a modern, fast alternative to HubSpot Marketing Hub, Customer.io,
ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, and Brevo — and an open-source successor to
Mautic and Listmonk, built on a 2026-grade, AI-first stack.

## Why Helio

Marketing automation today forces a bad choice:

- **HubSpot, Customer.io, Klaviyo** are polished, but closed, expensive, and
  priced per contact. Real automation sits behind premium tiers, and your
  customer data lives in someone else's cloud.
- **Mautic** is powerful but heavy (PHP/Symfony, 4–8 GB RAM) with slowing
  community velocity.
- **Listmonk** is delightfully fast, but newsletters only — no journeys, no
  automation.

Helio takes the best of each: Listmonk's performance, Mautic's automation
depth, HubSpot's polish — open-source, self-hostable, data-sovereign, and
AI-native from the first commit rather than as a bolt-on.

## What you can do today

The end-to-end loop works now: bring contacts in, build a segment, design an
email, launch a campaign or an event-triggered journey, and watch opens and
clicks land in the dashboard.

- **Durable journeys.** Every journey runs on [Temporal](https://temporal.io):
  it survives a `kill -9` mid-wait without double-sending.
- **AI copilot.** Describe a segment, journey, or on-brand email in a sentence
  and get a working draft — all grounded in your own organization's data.
- **Agent-ready.** An [MCP server](/docs/api/mcp) exposes Helio's capabilities
  as tools, so external AI agents can drive campaigns programmatically.
- **Own your data.** Postgres with row-level security isolates every tenant at
  the database, not just in application code.
