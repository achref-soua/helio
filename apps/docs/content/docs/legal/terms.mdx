## The license is the contract

[GNU Affero General Public License v3.0](https://github.com/achref-soua/helio/blob/main/LICENSE).
The license — not this page — defines your rights to run, study, modify, and
redistribute the software, including the AGPL's network-use provision: if you
offer a modified Helio to others over a network, they are entitled to your
modified source. Nothing here narrows what the AGPL grants.

## No warranty

In plain words what AGPL §15–16 says formally: Helio is provided **"as is",
without warranty of any kind**. The maintainers are not liable for damages
arising from its use — including lost data, lost revenue, or undelivered
mail. You run it, you operate it, you back it up (the product
[helps with that](/docs/guides/backup-restore), but the responsibility is
yours).

## Acceptable use

Helio is marketing-automation software with consent built in: suppression
lists honored everywhere, one-click unsubscribe on every marketing email,
and double-opt-in tooling. Using Helio to send **unsolicited bulk mail or
messages that violate the laws of your recipients' jurisdictions** (GDPR,
ePrivacy, CAN-SPAM, CASL, and their kin) is a misuse of the software and of
this project's name. The project will not assist with it, and deliverability
itself will punish it.

## The Helio name and mark

You may say your product or service runs on Helio. You may not name a fork,
product, or service in a way that implies it **is** Helio or is endorsed by
the project. For your own brand on your own deployment, use the built-in
[white-labeling](/docs/guides/white-labeling) — that is what it is for.

## Releases and integrity

Release binaries, bundles, and container images are provided as a
convenience. Verify downloads against the `checksums.txt` published with
each release (the installer and CLI do this automatically). Assets are
only authentic if they come from the project's GitHub releases or
`ghcr.io/achref-soua/*`.

and you license it to the project under AGPL-3.0. See
[CONTRIBUTING](https://github.com/achref-soua/helio/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
for the workflow.
