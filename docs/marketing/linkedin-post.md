For the last while I've been building something I wanted to exist and couldn't find: an open-source, self-hostable, AI-native marketing-automation platform. It's called **Helio**, and it's live.

The problem: the great marketing tools (HubSpot, Customer.io, Klaviyo) are closed and bill per contact. The open ones are either aging or newsletter-only. None are built AI-first on a modern stack.

So Helio is:

→ A full growth platform — CDP, visual segmentation, cross-channel journeys, email + SMS + WhatsApp + web push, landing pages, analytics, and a light CRM.

→ AI-native — describe a segment, journey, or email in plain English and get a working draft you can preview before saving. Bring your own model, including a local one, so your data never leaves your network.

→ Durable — journeys run on Temporal, so they survive crashes and multi-week waits with no lost state and no double-sends.

→ Yours — free forever, self-hosted, no telemetry, no per-contact billing. One command to run on Docker.

It's AGPL-3.0 and built on Next.js, TypeScript, Python, PostgreSQL, and ClickHouse.

If you've ever been priced out of "real" automation, or you just want to own your customer data, I'd love your eyes on it — and a star helps more people find it.
